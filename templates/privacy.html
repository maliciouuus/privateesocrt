1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how EscortDollars (hereinafter "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information you provide when using our affiliate platform.

We are committed to respecting your privacy and handling your personal data with the utmost care and transparency.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Data you provide to us

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Identification information (first name, last name, username)
  • Contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Financial information (banking details for payments)
  • Telegram username and other contact information for verification
  • Information about your traffic sources and professional activity
  • Any content you voluntarily submit (messages, communications, etc.)

2.2 Automatically collected data

We also automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • IP address and approximate location data
  • Information about your device and browser
  • Usage and browsing data on our platform
  • Statistics on your affiliate activities
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2.3 Age verification

In accordance with our commitment to limit access to our platform to adults only:

  • We collect data to verify that you are over 18 years old
  • This may include a copy of your ID (which will be processed securely)
  • This data is processed solely for verification purposes and is stored securely

3. Use of Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

3.1 Contract performance

  • Creating and managing your affiliate account
  • Processing and tracking your affiliate activities
  • Calculating and paying your commissions
  • Communicating with you regarding our services

3.2 Legitimate interests

  • Improving and personalizing our services
  • Analyzing usage trends
  • Securing our platform
  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Verifying compliance with our Terms of Service

3.3 Legal obligations

  • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Age and identity verification
  • Responding to requests from competent authorities
  • Maintaining tax and accounting records

4. Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with:

4.1 Service providers

We work with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (payment processing, hosting, analytics, etc.). These providers only have access to the data necessary to perform their functions and are required to process it in accordance with our policy.

4.2 Business partners

As part of our affiliate program, certain information may be shared with our business partners to facilitate conversion tracking and commission calculations.

4.3 Legal authorities

We may disclose your information if we are legally required to do so or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation
  • Protect our rights or property
  • Prevent or investigate potential fraud or violations of our Terms of Service
  • Protect the personal safety of users or the public

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

The criteria used to determine our retention periods include:

  • The duration for which you have an active account on our platform
  • Legal obligations to which we are subject
  • Applicable limitation periods
  • Dispute resolution
  • Exercise or defense of legal claims

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Continuous monitoring of our systems to detect potential vulnerabilities
  • Training our staff on data protection issues

7. Your Rights

In accordance with the GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You have the right to obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You can request the deletion of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request to receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent: When processing is based on your consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address indicated at the end of this policy.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our platform uses cookies and other similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and personalize content.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our platform.

9. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When we make such transfers, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, in accordance with GDPR requirements.

10. Privacy Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised update date. We encourage you to regularly review this policy to stay informed about how we protect your data.

By using our service, you acknowledge that you have read and accepted this Privacy Policy in its entirety. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us.
